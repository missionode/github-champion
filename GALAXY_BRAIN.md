# 🌌 Achievement: Galaxy Brain

The **Galaxy Brain** badge is awarded for having your answers accepted in GitHub Discussions.

## 📋 Steps to Earn it:

1.  **Enable Discussions:**
    - Go to your repository settings on GitHub.com.
    - Under **General** > **Features**, check the **Discussions** box.
2.  **Seed a Question:**
    - Go to the **Discussions** tab in your repo.
    - Start a new discussion in the **"Q&A"** category.
    - Ask a technical question or a "How to?" question.
3.  **Provide an Answer:**
    - You can answer it yourself or ask a friend to help.
4.  **Mark as Answer:**
    - Click the **"Mark as answer"** checkmark on the best comment.

### 🏆 Tiers:
- **Tier 1:** 2 Answers accepted.
- **Tier 2 (Bronze):** 8 Answers.
- **Tier 3 (Silver):** 16 Answers.
- **Tier 4 (Gold):** 32 Answers.
